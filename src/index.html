A Tribute to Artistic Legacy

Mai Mai Hotel draws inspiration from Indonesia’s vibrant cultural spirit, honoring the nation's rich artistic heritage through its design and atmosphere. One of our proudest influences is the work of legendary Indonesian artist Hendra Gunawan—celebrated for his vivid portrayals of daily life, historic moments, and the colorful soul of the Indonesian people. Born in Bandung in 1918, Gunawan was more than a painter—he was a poet and sculptor who helped shape the course of modern Indonesian art during the early years of independence. His distinctive style, full of flowing motion, expressive forms, and bold color, resonates throughout our interiors. At Mai Mai Hotel, his legacy lives on through artful spaces that echo his love for nature, rural life, and the human spirit—woven into every curve, color, and detail of our design.
